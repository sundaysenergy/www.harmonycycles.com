ABOUT US

Specializing in bicycles for your everyday needs, this Seward neighborhood bike shop is committed to making all of your bicycle journeys a pleasure. Whether you need a bike to carry your children, pets, plants, groceries, or simply need something stylish to transport your fetching self, we'll help you get it.

Meet the team

Tim Lupfer

Co-Founder

I love bicycles. I do not own a car, so naturally, transportational cycling is an important part of my life. However, it does not define my experience. I also bike for sport, recreation, self examination/introspection, and the pure childish joy of being on a bicycle. I love the dirt, I love the snow, and I love the asphalt. It’s all perfect and worth experiencing. My girlfriend and I own an old three speed tandem. It is often used for date night. This is one of my favorite things in life. Other hobbies and interests include: sewing, reading, writing, food, wine, beer, conversation, photography, organization, doodling, wordgames, board games, feet games, ball games, hiking, running, camping, interesting smells, music, movies, and uhhh… stuff!
