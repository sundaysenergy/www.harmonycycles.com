Welcome to Harmony Cycles

Offering a different approach to the bike shop experience, we narrow the focus to the two things that matter--you and your bike. By utilizing a service oriented, appointment only scheduling model, we are able to offer the best pricing, quality, and turnaround time for your bicycle tuneup or repair.

ABOUT US

Specializing in bicycles for your everyday needs, this Seward neighborhood bike shop is committed to making all of your bicycle journeys a pleasure. Whether you need a bike to carry your children, pets, plants, groceries, or simply need something stylish to transport your fetching self, we'll help you get it.

Meet the team

Tim Lupfer

Advisor and mechanic

Seven years working in the bicycle industry has taught me to appreciate the infinite capacity, accessibility, and scalability of the bicycle. I do not own a car, so transportational cycling is an important part of my life. I also ride for sport, recreation, self-examination/introspection, and the pure, childish joy of being on a bicycle. Dirt, snow, or asphalt--they're all perfect. Aw, love.

Kai Curry

Founder

Biking is an amazing experience. It's the closest I can get to flying around like superman without leaving the ground. Professionally I've ventured from ecologist to technologist. Since staring at screens all day isn't so great I now have scheduled time to work with my hands and fuel my passion for self-supporting transportation. Besides, how else was I going to get a Winther Kangaroo for my baby girl? Go bikes!

Bryce Rasmussen

Mechanic and chemistry teacher

Living in the city and working in the suburbs gives me an unusual insight into the challenges of bike commuting in all situations. With a daily round trip commute of 40+ miles, I have loads of experience with everything bike-commuting and public transportation related. My most memorable commutes involve lots of precipitation, busses, bikes, trains, 20mph headwinds, on-the road repairs, and 3 hour one-way commute times.

Alex Cook

Framebuilder, mechanic, and visionary leader

I have about five years of experience as a framebuilder, going back to my initial education at UBI. I focus on creating handcrafted bicycles and accessories that are organically suited to a rider's goals, temperament and aesthetic sensibilities. While this has transformed into a business, it was born out of a passion and love for cycling. In my spare time, I help out at Harmony.
